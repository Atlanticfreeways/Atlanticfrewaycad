## FAQs

**Q: Is this a real bank?**  
A: No, we're a fintech platform. We partner with licensed banks to hold funds and issue cards via Marqeta.

**Q: How long does KYC take?**  
A: With automated KYC (Onfido), it takes 2-5 minutes. Manual review takes 24-48 hours.

**Q: Can I use this for business expenses?**  
A: Yes! We have dedicated business plans with team management and expense tracking.

**Q: What if my card is stolen?**  
A: Freeze it instantly in the app. Since we use JIT funding, there's no balance on the card to steal.

**Q: Do you support crypto?**  
A: Yes! Turbo and Business users can fund wallets with Bitcoin (BTC) and Ethereum (ETH).

**Q: What are the fees?**  
A: No monthly fees for personal accounts. Physical card costs $5. Currency conversion is 0.5% (fiat) or 2% (crypto).

**Q: Is my money FDIC insured?**  
A: Yes, funds are held at our partner bank and insured up to $250,000 per account.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**  
A: Yes, refunds are processed by merchants and appear in your wallet within 5-10 business days.
